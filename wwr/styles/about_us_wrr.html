Dry Oar

Dry Oar was founded in 2000 by the Hall family who had a passionate for adventurers who wanted to share their love of white water rafting with others. With a deep appreciation for the natural beauty of the mountains that surrounded them on their family estate; they wanted to share the beauty with the world. The 9000 acres of land of land was then recreated into a scenic nature park offering unforgettable experiences for people of all ages. We have become one of the major excursion touring company that pride its self with top-of-the-line equipment and hired a team of experts and guides who are trained in first aid and water so as to ensure a fun and safe environment. Today, Dry Oar is a thriving offering a range of trips that cater to the needs and desires of all adventurers. Come and enjoy the beauty of the mountains and the excitement of white-water rafting.
