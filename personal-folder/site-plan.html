Overview

Purpose

Have you ever wished or wondered if you can submit a service request ticket from the comfort of your office without having to leave your work area? We understand the negative impact this can pose on businesses since many maintenance or system requests are done manually, this can have a negative impact on the business such as delayed resolution time which can reduce productivity within the organization. For this reason, we have created our services which is based on third-party ticketing system that is designed with you in mind—to help your company log and track service requests to specified team member/s, where the tickets can be handled sequentially or based on their level of importance.

Audience

RemotelyLog, Inc supports small-scale enterprises and employees, as we understand how frustrating it can be to have limited options in the workplace. Let us help you satisfy your company’s needs!
